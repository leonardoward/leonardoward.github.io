My name is Leonardo Ward and I have a Bachelor's Degree in Electronics Engineering. I have experience developing consumer and industrial products: I design the schematics and the PCB layouts (with Altium Designer and KiCAD). I coordinate the manufacture process directly with the suppliers and PCB manufacturers, in Europe and China. Finally, I design the firmware for those products in C++ and/or python.
