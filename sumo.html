ATLAS, a Robot for Sumo Competitions

  • October, 2016
  • By Leonardo Ward

Design and Construction of a Mobile Robot for the Venezuelan National Competition USBBOTS 2016. The robot prototype uses brushless dc motors, infrared laser and ultrasonic distance sensors.

The Motors

There were used two recicled brushless DC motors. There wasn't found a complete diagram or datasheet for the board, therefore the team reverse engineered the board.

Components:

  • SLA6023 : 3-phase motor driver (60V 6A 25W).
  • LB1823 : predriver IC developed for driving power brushless motors in office automation applications.
